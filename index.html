When to Use This Template

Use this onboarding request template only when a service provider is sourced directly by a Care Partner (SAH) or Service Manager (NDIS), rather than through the Recruitment team.

A service provider is any individual or organisation engaged to deliver services that is not directly employed â including independent contractors, sole traders, and subcontracted workers.

Implementation

1. Self-Managed Services (SAH only â not applicable to NDIS)

  • Care Partners must ensure that services MUST NOT commence until onboarding is fully completed.
  • The compliance team will complete the onboarding and notify the relevant Care Partner once onboarding is finalised.
  • Services may only commence after confirmation from the compliance team.

2. Provider Managed Services (Applicable to SAH & NDIS)

  • Care Partners/Case Managers should prioritise using existing approved resources wherever possible before sourcing new ones.
  • All new resources must be approved prior to onboarding:
    • Home Care (Melbourne & Sydney): approval required from Aneta
    • NDIS: approval required from Harry
  • If a new resource is identified: proposed pricing must not exceed 80% of standard rates, using the current home care price list. For NDIS, use the pricing guide. Exceptions can be made but must be approved by Aneta or Harry.
  • Care Partners/Case Managers must confirm approval directly with the relevant approver before submitting any onboarding request.
  • Once approval is obtained, proceed to creating a task on Visual Care.
  • The Compliance team will complete onboarding and notify the relevant Care Partner/Case Manager once finalised. Services MUST NOT commence until onboarding is fully completed.
